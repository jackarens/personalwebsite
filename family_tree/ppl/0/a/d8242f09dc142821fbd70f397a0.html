== Biography ==
Walter Stewart, 6th High Steward of Scotland was born in 1292, the son of [[Stewart-1015|James Stewart]], 5th High Steward of Scotland and Gille de Burgh. <span style="text-decoration:underline;color:red;"><span style="color:black;">He married Alice Erskine, daughter of Sir John Erskine, before 1315.</span></span> <span style="color:red;">Considered doubtful by most authorities.</span> He married [[Bruce-124|Margorie Bruce]], Princess of Scotland, daughter of Robert I Bruce, King of Scotland and Isabella of Mar, in 1315. He married, [[Graham-820|Isabella Graham]], daughter of Sir John Graham of Abercorn and Mary of Strathearn. He died on 9 April 1327.
