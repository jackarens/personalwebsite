== Biography ==Sir Philip le Despenser was the son of Hugh "the elder" (not the younger Despenser as sometimes stated) and his wife Isabel de Beauchamp. He was still a minor in 1294.
He married Margaret, daughter of Sir Ralph de Goushill of Camoys manor and his wife Hawise FitzWarin.
They had one son, who they called Philip, starting an unimaginative trend.

Sir Philip (sr) died on 24 Sep 1313.
