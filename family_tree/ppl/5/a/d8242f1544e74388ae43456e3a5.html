Narrative

== Biography ==John was the 4th Lord Somerville. He married [[Baillie-28|Marion]], daughter of WIlliam Baillie of Lamington.<ref name=Burke>Bernard Burke, ''A Genealogical and Heraldic History of the Landed Gentry of Great Britain & Ireland, Volume 1'' (London: Harrison and Sons, 1898), p. 917, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=YUtNAQAAMAAJ&lpg=PA917. Google Books (http://books.google.com : accessed 25 June 2016).</ref>
He died in November 1491 at Cowthally Castle, Carnwath, Lanarkshire and was buried at St Mary's Aisle, Carnwath, Lanarkshire.
