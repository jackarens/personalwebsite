John of Strathbogie, 9th Earl of Atholl was the son of David of Strathbogie, 8th Earl of Atholl and Isabel of Chilham. He married Margaret of Mar, daughter of Donald, 6th Earl of Mar and Helen ferch Llywelyn.1 He died on 7 November 1306 at London, England, executed, with his body burnt and his head fixed on London Bridge (and his titles forfeited)
