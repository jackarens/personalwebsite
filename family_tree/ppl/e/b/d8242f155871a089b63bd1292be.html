==Biography of Janet Mowat==
*Janet Mowat was the daughter of Sir John Mowat. She married William Somerville, 1st Lord Somerville, son of Sir Thomas de Somerville and Janet Stewart, in 1428/29 in a secret marriage, against his parent's wishes. She died between 1456 and 1458 at Cowthally Castle, Lanarkshire, Scotland. From 1428/29, her married name became Somerville.
