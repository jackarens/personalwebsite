== Biography ==
The son of Robert de Boyt (Boyd) 1st Feudal Baron of Kilmarnock (1249 to 1333) and Brother of1. Sir Thomas (The Archer) 2nd Feudal Baron of Kilmarnock Boyd b. c13232. Sir Alan Boyd killed at the seige of Perth while in Command of the Scottish Archery.James de Boyd was born 1324 and he witnessed a charter in 1342.
