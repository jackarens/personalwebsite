== Biography of Herbert Herries ==

*Sir Herbert Herries, Baron Carlaverock, d. 4 July 1440

*Father Sir John Herries, Sire de Trevereglys d. 27 Mar 1420

*Mother Euphemia Lindsay d. a 1405
*Sir Herbert Herries, Baron Carlaverock married Margaret Douglas, daughter of Sir Archibald Douglas, 3rd Earl of Douglas, Earl of Wigtown, Lord of Galloway & Bothwell and Joanna Moray. Sir Herbert Herries, Baron Carlaverock died on 4 July 1440.
*Hebert had four brothers James, Robert, William and Sir John. He also had a daughter who married Herbert, 1st Lord Maxwell.
*He was knighted on May 23, 1424, at the coronation of James I. He also was called Herbert "Heyres" of Carlaverock.
*He died on July 4, 1440, leaving his widow Margaret, daughter of the "Earle Douglas" and four sons.
