== Biography ==

*[[Image: Paula s Sources-19.jpg|175pxl*]] '''Margaret Douglas'''

 

*The following information shows that there is conflicting family connections in this profile, since I am not the manager of the other family components I am adding information provided by a Douglas family decendant Sally Douglas.
*The following was sent via the private messaging system on WikiTree.com.
*I have done quite a bit of work on this Margaret Douglas and I have her marrying Thomas Johnson/Johnston the son of John. (If she is the Margaret Douglas - daughter of William Douglas the first Earl and his mistress Margaret Stewart, Countess of Angus).They kept the name of Douglas and Margaret was the first Laird or Lord of Bonjedward. Their son John Douglas was the second Laird or Lord. In a Latin charter it shows that her sister Isabel or Isabella Douglas handed the unentailed lands on Bonjedward to 'her dear sister' Margaret/Margarete Douglas the wife of Thomas Johnson.

*Thank You Sally.
*Margaret/Margarette Douglas - 1st Laird of Bonjedward c1376 (Bonjedburgh) [included the lands of Timpendean] and Thomas Johnson/Johnsone c1366
