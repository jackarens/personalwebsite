==Biography==

== Robert the Bruce (1274-1329) ==
: With the defeat of the English at the [[:Category: Battle of Bannockburn|Battle of Bannockburn]], Robert the Bruce secured Scottish sovereignty. Until James VI and I ascended the throne, his descendants would rule Scotland for three hundred years.<ref name=ash>Ashley, Mike (2008). ''A Brief History of British Kings & Queens''. pp.158-161, 460-461. Philadelphia: Running Press Book Publishers. Print</ref>
