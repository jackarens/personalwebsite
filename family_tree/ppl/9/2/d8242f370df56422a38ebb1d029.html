==Biography==
Lora de Vere married Reginald (Reynold) d' Argentine, son of Sir Giles d' Argentine, Gov. of Windsor Castle, circa 1270; They had 1 son (Sir John).2,3,5 Lora de Vere died in 1292; Initially buried at Little Wymondley Priory, Hertfordshire. Later removed to Baldock, Hertfordshire.
