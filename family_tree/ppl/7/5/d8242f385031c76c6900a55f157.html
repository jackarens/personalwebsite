== Biography ==

Egidia Douglas was the daughter of [[Douglas-388|William Douglas]]. She was known as The Fair Maid of Nithsdale.<ref name=Wikipedia>''Wikipedia'', (http:www.wikipedia.com: accessed 26 July 2015), "William Douglas of Nithsdale," https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/William_Douglas_of_Nithsdale. </ref>

=== Marriages ===She first married [[Sinclair-378|Henry Sinclair]] before 17 November 1407. They had one child, [[Sinclair-376|William Sinclair]]

Her second husband was Sir Alasdair Stewart.
