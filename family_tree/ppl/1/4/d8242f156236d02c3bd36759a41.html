== Biography ==Marion was the daughter of William Baillie of Lamington. She was the wife of [[Somerville-62|John Somerville]].<ref name=Burke>Bernard Burke, ''A Genealogical and Heraldic History of the Landed Gentry of Great Britain & Ireland, Volume 1'' (London: Harrison and Sons, 1898), p. 917, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=YUtNAQAAMAAJ&lpg=PA917. Google Books (http://books.google.com : accessed 26 June 2016). He married [[Somerville-179|Marion the daughter of John, 4th Lord Somerville.</ref>
She was born in Carnwath, Lanarkshire, Scotland about 1430. She died about 1506 ate the age 75 or 76.{{citation needed}}
