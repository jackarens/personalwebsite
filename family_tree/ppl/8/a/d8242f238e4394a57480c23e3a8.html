==Biography==
Margaretha (b. 1436 – d. Simmern 2 november 1486). Zij is getrouwd met Frederik I (de Vrome) Hertog van Wittelsbach Paltsgraaf van Simmern (b. 24 april 1417 – d. Simmern 29 november 1480) op 16 augustus 1454 te Lobith, Gld., zij was toen 18 jaar oud.
'''Margaret of Guelders''' (1436 – 15 August 1486, Simmern) was daughter of Arnold, Duke of Guelders (14 July 1410, Egmond-Binnen, North Holland – 23 February 1473, Grave) and Catherine of Cleves (25 May 1417 – 10 February 1479).
Margaret married Frederick I, the Hunsrücker (19 November 1417 – 29 November 1480), Count Palatine of Simmern.
