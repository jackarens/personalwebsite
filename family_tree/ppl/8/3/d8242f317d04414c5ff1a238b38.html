=== Note ===: Also called Margaret of Flanders. With both her husbands, ancestors of Emperor Charles IV.

==Sources==
* '''Royal Ancestry by Douglas Richardson Vol. III page 187''''''MARGUERITE OF FLANDERS''', Countess of Flanders and Hainault, daughter and co-heiress, born in 1202. She married (1st) before 23 July 1212 '''BOUCHARD D'AVESNES''', He was born about 1175. They had three sons. Bouchard and his wife, Marguerite, were divorced in 1221; he died in 1244. Marguerite married (2nd) shortly before 15 Nov. 1223 '''GUILLAUME DE DAMPIERRE''', son of Guy II de Dampierre, by Mathilde, daughter of Archambaud [VII]. They had three sons, and two daughters. GUILLAUME DE DAMPIERRE died 3 Sept. 1231. His widow, Marguerite, Countess of Flanders and Hainault, died 10 Feb. 1280.
