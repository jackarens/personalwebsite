Narrative

== Biography ==: Christian, daughter of Alan FitzRoland, aka Alan of Galloway, by Margaret Huntingdon his wife, married William de Forz, Count of Aumale, before April 1236. <ref name="Medieval">''Alan of Galloway 6. Christian''</ref> <ref>''Royal Ancestry: A Study in Colonial and Medieval Families'', vol I, page 228</ref> They had no issue. <ref name="Medieval"/>
