== Biography ==
Alice Gifford (Born 1345, in Yester, Peebles-Shire, Scotland) She was the second daughter of Sir Hugh Giffard, (Gifford) of Yester. She died after 1410 By this union (Sir Thomas) acquired a large fortune.
They had one son: Sir Thomas Boyd, "dnus de Kylmornowe", (died 1432) who was one of the sureties, or hostages, for King James I (who had been held by the English as prisoner for 19 years).
