== Biography ==
Janet Forrester was born about 1344. She was a daughter of Sir John "Libertine" Forrester, Lord of Corstorphine and Jean Sinclair.
Sister of John Forrester, 3rd Laird of Corstorphine and Unknown Forrester

=== Marriage===

Wife of Lord Robert Maxwell with whom she had four Children.

===Children===

* 1. John Maxwell, 3rd Lord Maxwell.

* 2. Agnes Compton.

* 3. Aymer Maxwell.

* 4. Thomas Maxwell.
