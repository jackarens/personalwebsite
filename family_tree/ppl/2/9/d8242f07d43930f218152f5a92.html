==Biography==

===Name===
Angharad ferch Llywelyn,<ref name="FMG"> Cawley, Foundation for Medieval Genealogy </ref><ref name="Richardson"> Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, V, 298 </ref>

===Birth and Parentage===
Angharad, born circa 1210, died 1257, was a daughter of Llywelyn ap Iorwerth. by an unknown mistress <ref name=FMG /> or "allegedly" Llywelyn's wife Joan. <ref name="Richardson"/>
Angharad may have been Joan's dau. Her illegitimacy is uncertain. See Wikipedia [[Wikipedia: Angharad ferch Llywelyn]]

===Marriage===
She maried [[Fychan-1|Maelgwn Fychan]] , Lord of Ceredigion [Cardigan], son of Maelgwyn ap Rhys, Lord of Ceredigion & his wife. <ref name="FMG"/>

===1257 Death===

She died in 1257. <ref name="FMG"/>
