== Biography ==[[Stewart-1515 | Elizabeth Isabel (Stewart) Campbell]] was (possibly) born in approximately 1419 to [[Stewart-581|John "Mourach" Stewart]], of Lorn, and [[MacDonald-436|Agnes the Isles MacDonald]]. Elizabeth married [[Campbell-1526 | Colin Campbell]], of Lochawe, 2nd Lord Lorne, 1st Earl of Argyll during or prior to April of 1465. She is also listed as having been married to Allen MacDonald, and had a child, Mora MacDonald, but Mora's profile shows that she was born LONG before her purported mother! It may be that Allen and Mora are relatives of Elizabeth's mother, Agnes of the Isles. As always, research is on-going, and input is always welcome! [[Felch-7 | RC]]

Their children were:

: [[Campbell-1525 | Archibald Campbell]], 2nd Earl of Argyll, b. 1449
: Mary Campbell, of Argyll, b. 1451
: [[Campbell-1508 | Thomas Campbell]], of Lundie, b. Abt 1456: [[Campbell-2321 |Isabel Campbell]], b. 1459, Lochow, Argyllshire, Scotland : [[Campbell-3281 | Helen Campbell]], b. Abt 1460, Lochow, Argyllshire, Scotland
: [[Campbell-1509 | Margaret Campbell]]
: [[Campbell-2822 | Elizabeth Campbell]]
: [[Campbell-1896| Catherine Campbell]]
