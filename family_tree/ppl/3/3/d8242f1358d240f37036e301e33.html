: Or Joan Douglas. : Stirnet.com: Although TCP (Dacre) [?] acknowledges that William's wife is said to have been Joan Douglas "illegit. da. of James, Earl of Douglas" it then reports that his wife was called Mary. We therefore provisionally show his wife as Douglas, possibly natural half-sister of James Douglas, 2nd Earl of Douglas, Earl of Mar.
