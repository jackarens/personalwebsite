Narrative

== Biography ==Jean, heiress of the Isles of Bute and Arran, daughter of James (who with his father and brothers was killed 1210 by the men of Skye), son of Angus Lord of Bute & Arran (younger son of Somerled, King of the South Isles). [Burke's Peerage]

About Jean Macrory, Heiress of Bute-Arran

Jean Macrory of Bute
Father James Macrory Lord of Bute and Arran b. circa 1185, d. 1210
Mother Daughter of the Arran of the Isles2 Name Variation Jean Macrory of Bute was also found as Jane of Bute.
Birth circa 1210

Married
Alexander Stewart 4th High Steward of Scotland, son of Walter Stewart 3rd High Steward of Scotland and Beatrix of Angus. No date for this marriage is given in Burke or by Thompsett, so have to assume that it occurred before the birth of first child. He was b. circa 1214, d. circa 1283

Children include

1. Hawise Stewart 2. Married John de Soulis. 2. Beatrix Stewart Countess of Crawford+ 2,6. also found as "daughter Stewart.". Married Sir Alexander Lindsay of Crawford and Luffness. 3. Sir John Stewart of Bonkyl+ b. c 1240, d. 22 Jul 1298 2. Married Margaret de Bonkyl. 4. James Stewart 5th High Steward of Scotland+ b. 1243, d. 16 Jul 1309. Married Gilles de Burgh. 5. Elizabeth Stewart+ b. c 1250 4. Married Sir William "Le Hardi" Douglas of that Ilk.
