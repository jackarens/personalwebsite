== Biography ==
Matthew Stuart, 4th Earl of Lennox (1516-1571) was the son of John Stuart, 3rd Earl of Lennox and Lady Elizabeth Stewart. He married Lady Margaret Douglas, daughter of Archibald Douglas, 6th Earl of Angus and Lady Margaret Tudor, on 6 July 1544 at St. James's Palace, St. James's, London, England. He succeeded his father as 4th (or 14th) Earl of Lennox and 4th Lord Darnley in 1526. He died on 4 September 1571 at age 54 at Stirling Castle, Stirling, Stirlingshire, Scotland, wounded while a prisoner during a raid on Stirling Castle, and after his rescue.<ref>[https://archive.org/stream/completepeerage05cokahrish/TN-313027_5#page/n65/mode/2up The Complete Peerage 1st ed. v.4, p. 64]</ref>
