==Biography==
Margaret Boyd was born 1413 in Kilmarnock, Ayrshire, Scotland and died 1453 in Eaglesham, Renfrewshire, Scotland. She married Patrick Dunbar.<ref>Entered by Sheri Sturm.</ref>
Margaret Boyd<ref name="Mosley">Mosley, Charles, editor. Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, 3 volumes. Wilmington, Delaware, U.S.A.: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 2003. Volume 1, page 1279.</ref> was the daughter of Sir Thomas Boyd, 5th of Kilmarnock.<ref>G.E. Cokayne; with Vicary Gibbs, H.A. Doubleday, Geoffrey H. White, Duncan Warrand and Lord Howard de Walden, editors, The Complete Peerage of England, Scotland, Ireland, Great Britain and the United Kingdom, Extant, Extinct or Dormant, new ed., 13 volumes in 14 (1910-1959; reprint in 6 volumes, Gloucester, U.K.: Alan Sutton Publishing, 2000), volume VII, page 121. </ref>
She married Alexander Montgomerie, 1st Lord Montgomerie, son of Sir John Montgomerie of Ardrossan and Agnes of the Isles.<ref name="Cokayne">Cokayne, and others, The Complete Peerage, volume IX, page 135. </ref>

Her married name became Montgomerie.<ref name="Cokayne"> </ref>

She died after September 1453.<ref name="Cokayne"> </ref>
