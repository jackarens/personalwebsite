== Biography ==
'''Father''' Sir John Stewart, 1st Earl Atholl, Ambassador to England b. c 1440, d. 15 Sep 1512
'''Mother''' Margaret 'Fair Maid of Galloway' Douglas<ref>The Complete Peerage of England, Scotland, Ireland, Great Britain, and the United Kingdom, by George Edward Cokayne, Vol. VI, p. 99.</ref> d. bt 22 Jan 1473 - 19 Apr 1475

Elizabeth Stewart was born after 1460. She married Andrew Gray, 2nd Lord Gray, Sheriff of Forfar, Justice of Scotland, son of Patrick Gray, Master of Gray and Annabella Forbes, before 1483.
