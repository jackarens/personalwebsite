== Biography ==Muriel, ([1244]-[16 May/12 Nov] 1291), daughter of Malise, Earl of Strathearn and his first wife, Marjory de Muschamp, daughter of Robert de Muschamp of Wooler & his wife Isabel ---.
First, she married, after 1267, William, Earl of Mar, as his second wife. He probably died before Jan 1279
She married secondly (before Jan 1279, [divorced before 1291]) James, son of Alexander & his wife ---. It is possible that this is James Stewart, later High Steward of Scotland, son of ALEXANDER Stewart High Steward of Scotland & his wife Jean [of Bute] ([1243]-16 Jul 1309).]
