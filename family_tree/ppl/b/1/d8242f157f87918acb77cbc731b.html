== Biography ==
Elizabeth Keith married Sir Adam Gordon, Lord Gordon, son of Sir John Gordon and Elizabeth Keith, circa 1380; They had 2 daughters (Elizabeth, wife of Sir Alexander de Seton, Lord Gordon & (unnamed, who died young). Elizabeth Keith and Sir Thomas de Somerville, Lord Somerville, Justiciary South of the Forth obtained a marriage license on 2 November 1411; Dispensation for being related in the 3rd and 4th degrees of kindred. "Married anew." Elizabeth Keith died circa 1436.
