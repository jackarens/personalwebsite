Narrative

Eleanor of England (1306 - 1311), was the only daughter of Edward I and Margaret of France. Her biological siblings are Thomas Brotherton, and Edmund of Woodstock, 1st Earl of Kent.<ref>[[Wikipedia: Margaret of France, Queen of England]]</ref>
(Royal Ancestry) Eleanor of England, born 4 May 1306. She was buried at Beaulieu Abbey, Hampshire, shortly before 28 August 1310.
