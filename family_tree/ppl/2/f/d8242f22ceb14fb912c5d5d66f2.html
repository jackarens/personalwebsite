== Biography ==: '''Father''' Sir Richard FitzAlan, 9th Earl of Surrey, 10th Earl Arundel, Sheriff of Shropshire b. c 1314, d. 24 Jan 1376

: '''Mother''' Isabel le Despenser b. c 1313, d. a 1356
: Sir Edmund FitzAlan was born circa 1329; Age 18 in 1347. He married Sibyl de Montague, daughter of Sir William de Montacute, 1st Earl of Salisbury, 3rd Lord Montagu, Earl Marshal of England and Katharine de Grandison, before 16 July 1347; They had 3 daughters (Elizabeth, wife of Sir Richard Carew, Baron Carew; Philippe, wife of Sir Richard Sergeaux, & of Sir John Cornwall, Lord of Fanhope; & Katherine, wife of Sir Robert Deincourt). Sir Edmund FitzAlan died before 12 February 1382 at of Bignor, Trayford, & Compton, Sussex, England; Also of Chudleigh, Devonshire; Melbury Bubb, Dorsetshire; & Chedzoy, Martock, Sutton Montagu, & Thurlbear, Somersetshire.
