== Biography ==
Margaret's father is [[Hepburn-382|Adam Hepburn]] and her mother is [[Borthwick-150|Janet De Borthwick]].
Margaret was married to [[Cunningham-2577|Sir Alexander Cunningham]], Lords of Kilmaurs and Glencairn. <ref>Douglas, David. "Cunningham, Earl of Glencairn." The Scots Peerage. Vol. IV. Edinburgh: T and A Constable, 1907. 234. Print.</ref>
She is the daughter of Adam Hepburn, Lord of Hailes. <ref>Douglas, David. "Cunningham, Earl of Glencairn." The Scots Peerage. Vol. IV. Edinburgh: T and A Constable, 1907. 232-34. Print.</ref>
