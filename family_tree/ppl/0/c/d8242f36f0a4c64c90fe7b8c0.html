== Biography ==Edited notes from Douglas Richardson's Royal Ancestry and Magna Carta Ancestry:
Alphonse de Vere was the third son of Robert de Vere and Alice de Sanford, born before 1262. He married before 1301, Joan Foliot, daughter of Sir Jordan Foliot and Margery de Newmarch. They had one son, John, Knt., and one possible daughter, Margery. Sir Alphonse de Vere died shortly before 20 Dec 1328 and was buried at St. Albans Abbey, Hertfordshire, where he and his wife were benefactors.
