=== Biography ===

*Isabel of Lennox, Countess of Lennox;, b. circa 1370, d. circa 1458
*Isabel of Lennox, Countess of Lennox was born circa 1370.3 She was the daughter of Duncan, 8th Earl of Lennox and Helen Campbell.
*She married Murdoch Stewart, 2nd Duke of Albany, son of Robert Stewart, 1st Duke of Albany and Margaret Graham, Countess of Menteith, from 17 February 1391/92 to 8 November 1392. She died circa 1458 at Inchmurrin Castle, Loch Lomond, Scotland, without surviving issue.*She was also known as Elizabeth of.3 Her married name became Stewart. As a result of her marriage, Isabel of Lennox, Countess of Lennox was styled as Duchess of Albany on 3 September 1420.
*She gained the title of Countess of Lennox on 12 May 1437, suo jure.
