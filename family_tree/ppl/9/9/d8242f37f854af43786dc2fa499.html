== Biography ==
'''Father''' Sir William de Beauchamp, Earl of Warwick, 5th Baron Beauchamp, Sheriff of Worcester & Worcestershire b. 1215, d. c 25 Apr 1269

'''Mother''' Isabel Mauduit d. b 30 Jan 1268
Sir William de Beauchamp, 9th Earl Warwick, 6th Baron Beauchamp was born circa 1238 at of Elmley & Salwarpe, Worcestershire, England; Age 26 and 30 in 1268. He married Maud FitzJohn, daughter of Sir John FitzGeoffrey, Sheriff of Yorkshire, Justice of Ireland and Isabel Bigod, before 1270; They had 2 sons (John; & Sir Guy, 10th Earl of Warwick) and 3 daughters (Isabel, wife of Sir Patrick de Chaworth; Anne, nun at Shouldham; & Amy, nun at Shouldham). Sir William de Beauchamp, 9th Earl Warwick, 6th Baron Beauchamp left a will on 3 May 1297 or 14 September 1297.5 He died on 5 June 1298 or 9 June 1298 at Elmley, Worcestershire, England. He was buried on 22 June 1298 at Friars Minor, Worcester, Worcestershire, England.
