Narrative

== Biography ==Alice de Sanford was born circa 1230 at Great Hormede, Hertfordshire, England, the daughter of Gilbert de Saunford.<ref name="2magna">Douglas Richardson, Magna Carta Ancestry, 2nd Edition, Vol. IV, p. 263-264.</ref><ref name="3royal">Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. V, p. 253.</ref><ref name="4royal">Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. V, p. 312.</ref>

=== Marriage & Children ===:She married Sir Robert de Vere, 5th Earl Oxford, Baron of Hedingham & Whitchurch, Master Chamberlain of England, son of Sir Hugh de Vere, 4th Earl Oxford, Baron of Hedingham & Whitchurch, Master Chamberlain of England and Hawise de Quincy, before 22 February 1252. They had 6 sons & 2 daughters:<ref name="2magna"/><ref name="3royal"/><ref name="4royal"/>

# Sir Robert, 6th Earl of Oxford
# Sir Hugh, 1st Lord Vere
# Sir Alphonse
# Thomas
# Gilbert, a cleric
# Philip, a cleric
# Joan, wife of Sir William de Warenne
# Hawise

=== Death ===:She died on 7 September 1312 near Dunmon, Canfield, Essex, England; Buried at Earls Colne, Essex.<ref name="2magna"/><ref name="3royal"/>
