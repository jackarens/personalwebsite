== Biography ==
Janet Dunbar was the daughter of George Dunbar, 10th Earl of Dunbar and Beatrice (?).<ref name="Mosley">Mosley, Charles, editor. Burke's Peerage and Baronetage, 106th edition, 2 volumes. Crans, Switzerland: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 1999. Volume 1, page 80.</ref>
She married, firstly, Sir William Seton of Seton, son of Sir John Seton of Seton and Katherine Sinclair.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
She married, secondly, Sir Adam Johnstone of that Ilk, son of Sir John Johnstone, after 1433.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>Children with Adam Johnstone, Patrick, Archibald, William, born before 1455.
Her married name became de Seton,1 then after 1433, her married name became Johnstone.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
She died after 1434.<ref>G.E. Cokayne; with Vicary Gibbs, H.A. Doubleday, Geoffrey H. White, Duncan Warrand and Lord Howard de Walden, editors, The Complete Peerage of England, Scotland, Ireland, Great Britain and the United Kingdom, Extant, Extinct or Dormant, new ed., 13 volumes in 14 (1910-1959; reprint in 6 volumes, Gloucester, U.K.: Alan Sutton Publishing, 2000), volume XI, page 633. </ref>
