== Biography ==Agnes was the wife of [[Pollock-861|Robert de Pollok]] and daughter of Sir [[de_Maxwell-4|John Maxwell, Lord Caelaverock]].<ref name=Burke>Sir Bernard Burke, ''A Genealogical and Heraldic Dictionary of the Peerage and Baronetage of the British Empire, 30th Edition'' (London: Harrison, 1868), p. 887, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=K2FHAQAAMAAJ&pg=PA887 (http://books.google.com : accessed 31 July 2016).</ref> Metcalfe cites her father's name as John Maxwell of Mearns.<ref name=Metcalfe> William Musham Metcalfe, ''A History of the County of Renfrew from the Earliest Times'' (Paisley: Alexander Gardner, 1905), p. 95, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=OC8DAAAAYAAJ&pg=PA95 (http://books.google.com : accessed 31 July 2016).</ref>
