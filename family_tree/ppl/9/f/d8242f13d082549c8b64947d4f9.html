=Biography=
Thomas Stewart, 3rd Earl of Angus was the son of Thomas Stewart, 2nd Earl of Angus and Margaret Sinclair.

He died in 1377, young, unmarried.

He succeeded to the title of 3rd Earl of Angus circa 1361.
