== Biography of Thomas Randolph==
*Thomas Randolph married Isabel, only daughter of Sir John Stewart of Bonkyll (killed at the Battle of Falkirk (1298)), a brother of James Stewart, 5th High Steward of Scotland.

They had several children, including:
*Thomas Randolph, 2nd Earl of Moray
*John Randolph, 3rd Earl of Moray,
*Agnes Randolph, who married Patrick Dunbar, 9th Earl of Dunbar.*Geilis (or Isabella) Randolph, wife of John de Dunbar of Derchester and Birkynsyde, mother of George I, Earl of March
