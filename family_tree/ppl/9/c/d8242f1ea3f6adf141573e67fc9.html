Narrative

== Biography ==Prebend Hamilton held the wealthy prebend of Kincardine. He was said to have come from Some and Sanquhar branch of the house of Hamilton. Under the rules of the day, Hamilton could not have married. <ref>[https://archive.org/stream/familyofburnetto00burnuoft#page/19/mode/1up The family of Burnett of Leys, with collateral branches; page 19]</ref>
However he is noticed as having a natural daughter, although the name of the mother is not mentioned in source, Janet Hamilton.
