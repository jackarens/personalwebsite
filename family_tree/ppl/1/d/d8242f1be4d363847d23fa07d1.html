== Biography ==:The fifth child of [[Stewart-1338 | John Stewart]], 1st Earl of Lennox (2nd creation, 1488) and [[Montgomery-308 | Margaret Montgomery]], she was also known as Margaret.<ref>[[#S2]] ''The Peerage''</ref> Based on her marriage of about 1480 to [[Colquhoun-54 | Sir John Colquhoun]] of Luss her birth was by 1464. Based on the assumed birth date of her son, she died after 1495, probably well after.<ref> [[#S1]] ''Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage''</ref>
