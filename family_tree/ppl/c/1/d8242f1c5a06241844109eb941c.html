== Biography ==Margaret de Quincy was the eldest daughter and co-heiress of [[Quincy-235|Roger de Quincy]]. She was born before 1223, and inherited a sixth of the barony of Leicester, Leicestershire.<ref name="MCA">Magna Carta Ancestry</ref>
She married William De Ferrers, Fifth Earl of Derby before 1238. Their son, [[Ferrers-225|Robert], was born about 1239.<ref name="MCA"/><ref name="CP">The Complete Peerage</ref>
William died at Evington, near Leicester, on March 24th or 28th, 1254, and was buried at Merevale Abbey on 31 Mar. 1254.<ref name="MCA"/><ref name="CP"/>

Margaret died shortly before 12 Mar 1280/1.<ref name="MCA"/>
Margaret, wife of Sir William de Ferrers, was buried with him in Merevale Abbey, Warwickshire. http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=48920554
