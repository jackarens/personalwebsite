Narrative

== Biography ==: Walter de Lacy son and heir of Gilbert de Lacy by Isaabel le Bigod, his wife, married Rohese le Boteler, daughter of Thebaud le Boteler. They had no issue. He died between 15 May 1238 and 1241. <ref>''Royal Ancestry: A Study in Colonial and Medieval Families'', vol V, page 239</ref>
