The marriage was reputiated and they were declared divorced sometime in 1387; the divorce was then later declared void on 17 Oct 1389. She died in Oct 1411. <ref> [http://fmg.ac/Projects/MedLands/CHAMPAGNE%20NOBILITY.htm#_ftnref2236 Cawley, ''Medieval Lands'': Philippa de Coucy]</ref> <ref>[http://fmg.ac/Projects/MedLands/ENGLISH%20NOBILITY%20MEDIEVAL1.htm#RobertVereOxforddied1392 Cawley, ''Medieval Lands'': Robert de Vere]</ref>

=== Death ===:'''Died:''' Died 5 October 1382 in England. <ref name="Richardson"/> <ref name="CP Gynes">Cokayne. ''Complete Peerage'', vol. 6 p. 228.</ref>
:Date is the one given by Richardson. Cawley notes several other possibledates of death but does not give any sources including 15 Mar/4 May 1379 or 17 Jun/05 Oct 1382. <ref name="MedLands1"/> The Bedford section of ''Complete peerage'' stated she had died before May 1379. <ref>Cokayne. ''Complete Peerage'', vol. 2 page 70.</ref> However, in the Gynes section (Coucey family) the correct date is given. On 22 November 1382 orders were issued to the collectors of customs regarding a regarding a grant of land "late belonging to the King's aunt, Isabel, late Countess of Bedford, who d. 5 Oct. last past." On the same day executors were appointed to carry out the terms of the Countess' will. <ref name="CP Gynes"/>
