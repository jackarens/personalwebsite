== Biography ==

=== Biography of Janet Keith ===
*Janet Keith is the daughter of [[Keith-1271|Sir William Keith]] and [[Fraser-1477|Margaret Fraser]]. <ref>Entered by Sir William Arbuthnot, Tuesday, August 27, 2013.</ref> : Janet Keith of Galston (Galstoun)<ref name=fmg /><ref name=foster>Foster, 1847</ref><ref>Alias: Johanna{{citation needed}}</ref>Born about 1340 Galston, Ayrshire, Scotland<ref name=gedcoms>[[#S258]] familysearch.org 3-28-2008; b. 1340 or 1342 (?); See gedcoms in changes.{{citation needed}}</ref>
*She married, firstly, Sir David Hamilton, 3rd of Cadzow, son of Sir David fitz Walter, 2nd of Cadzow and Margaret Leslie, before 1370.
*She married, secondly, Sir Alexander Stewart, son of Sir Alexander Stewart, circa 1381.
*She was also known as Joan and Johanna. From before 1370, her married name became Hamilton. From circa 1381, her married name became Stewart.
