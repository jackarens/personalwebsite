Narrative

== Biography ==Maud FitzAlan was born in 1263, <ref name="DP">Entered by Darrell Parker, Tuesday, October 15, 2013.</ref> or about 1275 in Arundel, Sussex, England.<ref>FamilySearch.org - http://www.familysearch.org</ref>
Maud was the sister of Richard Fitz Alan, Earl of Arundel.<ref name="SC8522570">The National Archives Website: Discovery: SC 8/52/2570, PETITIONS TO THE KING; TO THE KING AND COUNCIL; TO THE COUNCIL; TO THE PARLIAMENT; AND THE LIKE. http://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/C9062785, 6 November 2014. ''The original document can be viewed online.''</ref>
Maud married Philip Burnel, and Maud's brother Richard gave in free marriage to Maud and Philip tenements in several places including Acton Piggott in Shropshire.<ref name="SC8522570" />

Maud and Philip had children:
#Maud who was married to John de Handlo;<ref name="SC8522570" />
After Philip's death, Maud leased the tenements her brother gave them to Edmund Earl of Arundel.<ref name="SC8522570" />
In 1302, Maud Burnel, the widow of Philip Burnel, petitioned the king and council for remedy as her late husband held socages and burgages in various counties when he died, and she ought to have them for the upbringing of Edward, Philip's son, who is under age, but the king has retained the properties.<ref name="SC8313E63">The National Archives Website: Discovery: SC 8/313/E63, PETITIONS TO THE KING; TO THE KING AND COUNCIL; TO THE COUNCIL; TO THE PARLIAMENT; AND THE LIKE. http://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/C9529114, 6 November 2014. ''The original document can be viewed online.''</ref>

Maud died after 1302.<ref name="SC8313E63" />
