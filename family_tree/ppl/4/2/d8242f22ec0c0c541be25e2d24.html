==Biography==: John, Marshal of England in 1377, summoned to parliament 1st to 3rd Richard II [1377-1379]. He died 1379, having married Eleanor, grand-daughter and co-heir of John, Lord Maltravers, in whose right he bore that title, and by her (who m.2 Reginald, Lord Cobham), had issue, John, William, Thomas (also called Edward), Henry, and Joan.<ref>Burke, B. (1883). Dormant, Abeyant, Forfeited, and Extinct Peerages, Burke's Peerage, Ltd. (pp.201) London: n.p. [Fitz-Alan, Earls of Arundel, Barons Maltravers]</ref><ref>Ancestral Roots of Certain American Colonists, 7th Edition, by Frederick Lewis Weis, additions by Walter Lee Shippard Jr., 1999 </ref>
He was a great great grandson of King Henry III through his son Edmund Earl of Lancaster.

=== Marriage ===:: John married 17 Feb 1358/1359 Salisbury, Wiltshire, Eleanor, Baroness Mautravers in her own right. She married 2nd 9 Aug 1380, as his 2nd wife, Lord (Baron) Cobham (of Sterborough) and died 12 Jan 1404/5. Eleanor is the younger daughter of Sir John Mautravers and granddaughter of 1st Lord (Baron) Mautravers. <ref>''Magna Carta Ancestry: A Study in Colonial and Medieval Families'', vol I, page 30</ref>
