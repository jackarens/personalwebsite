== Biography ==Gilbert de Saunford, Lord Saunford was born at Great Hormede, Hertfordshire, England.
He married Lora (Lorette) Zouche, daughter of Roger le Zouche, 2nd Lord Zouche, Sheriff of Devonshire and Margaret Biset.

He died in 1249 at Woolston, Essex, England.
