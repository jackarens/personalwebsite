== Biography ==
John married (Papal dispensation 21 Nov 1348) as her first husband, Margaret Graham Countess of Menteith, daughter of John Graham & his wife Mary Countess of Menteith.<ref>Scots Peerage</ref><ref>FMG</ref>
John died in exile between April 20 and September 5, 1351. He didn't produce an heir so the title (Lord of Bothwell) passed to his younger brother Thomas.<ref>Scots Peerage</ref><ref>FMG</ref>
