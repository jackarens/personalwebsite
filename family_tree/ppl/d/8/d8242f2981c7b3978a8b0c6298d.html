==Biography==:Isabel de Beauchamp, daughter of Sir William de Beauchamp, 9th Earl Warwick, 6th Baron Beauchamp and Maud FitzJohn,<ref name="OurHugh1">Source [[#OurHugh1]]: [http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p368.htm#i11056 Sir Hugh le Despenser, Earl of Winchester], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors and Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref> was born circa 1268 at Stoke, Northamptonshire, England.<ref name="Our1">Source [[#Our1]]: [http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p124.htm#i3707 Isabel de Beauchamp], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors & Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref>
:She married Sir Patrick de Chaworth, Lord Kedwelly, son of Sir Patrick de Chaworth and Hawyse de London, circa 1276 at of Kempsford, Gloucestershire, England; They had 1 daughter ([[Chaworth-7|Maud]], wife of [[Plantagenet-129|Sir Henry Plantagenet]], Earl of Lancaster & Leicester, Lord of Monmouth).
:Sir Patrick "died testate shortly before 7 July 1283."<ref name="MCA">Source [[#MCA]]: Richardson, ''Magna Carta Ancestry'' (2011)</ref>
:Isabel then married [[Despenser-19|Hugh le Despenser]], 1st Earl of Winchester (1262-1326),<ref name="Wikipedia">Wikipedia: [http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Le_Despenser le Despenser]</ref> Constable of Devizes, Marlborough, Odiham, St. Briavel's, & Striguil Castles.<ref name="OurHugh1">Source [[#OurHugh1]]: [http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p368.htm#i11056 Sir Hugh le Despenser, Earl of Winchester], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors & Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref> He was born March 1, 1260/1 to [[Despenser-38|Sir Hugh le Despenser]], 1st Lord Despenser, Justiciar of England and [[Basset-79|Aliva (Aline) Basset]].<ref>The Peerage: [http://thepeerage.com/p1077.htm#i10761 Hugh le Despenser, 1st and last Earl of Winchester]</ref><ref>The Peerage: [http://thepeerage.com/p40672.htm#i406713 Sir Hugh le Despenser, 1st Lord Despenser]</ref>
:Hugh and Isabel married "between 10 Sept. 1285 (record of Highworth Hundred Rolls) and 27 Jan. 1287 (date of fine for marrying without license)."<ref name="MCA" />
:Hugh le Despenser and Isabel (Beauchamp) had 2 sons and 4 daughters:<ref name="MCA" />* [[Despenser-6|Sir Hugh, 2nd Lord Despenser]]<ref name="MCA" /><ref name="OurHugh1" />* [[Despenser-12|Sir Philip]],<ref name="OurHugh1" /> husband of [[Goushill-4|Margaret de Goushill]]<ref name="MCA" />* [[Despenser-14|Aline]], wife of [[Burnel-1|Sir Edward, Lord Burnell]]<ref name="OurHugh1" />* [[Despenser-13|Isabel]], wife of [[Clare-334|Gilbert de Clare]], of [[Hastings-1246|Sir John, 1st Lord Hastings]], & of [[Monthermer-2|Sir Ralph, 1st Lord Monthermer]]<ref name="OurHugh1" />* [[Despencer-158|Margaret]], wife of [[St_Amand-1|Sir John, 2nd Lord St. Amand]]<ref name="OurHugh1" />* [[Despenser-49|Elizabeth]], wife of [[Camoys-8|Sir Ralph, Lord Camoys]]<ref name="OurHugh1" />
