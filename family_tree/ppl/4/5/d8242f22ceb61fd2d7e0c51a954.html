== Biography ==

'''Father''' Sir John de Beaumont, 2nd Lord Beaumont, Earl of Buchan<ref>Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry, p. 84-85.</ref> b. c 1318, d. 14 Apr 1342
'''Mother''' Eleanor Plantagenet<ref>Douglas Richardson, Magna Carta Ancestry, 2nd Edition, Vol. I, p. 157-158.</ref> b. c 1312, d. 11 Jan 1372
Sir Henry de Beaumont, 3rd Lord Beaumont was born in 1340 at Brabant, Belgium; Age 2 in 1342. He married Margaret de Vere, daughter of Sir John de Vere, 7th Earl of Oxford, Master Chamberlain of England and Maud de Badlesmere, before 15 February 1363; They had 4 sons (Sir John, 4th Lord Beaumont; Henry; Richard; & Thomas) and 1 probable daughter (Eleanor/Elizabeth, wife of Sir Richard de Moleyns).<ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. IV, p. 107.</ref><ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. V, p. 259.</ref>Sir Henry de Beaumont, 3rd Lord Beaumont died on 13 June 1369 at of Folkingham, Barton on Humber, Edenham, & Heckington, Lincolnshire, England; Buried at Sempringham Priory, Lincolnshire.<ref> Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry, p. 85.</ref><ref> Douglas Richardson, Magna Carta Ancestry, 2nd Edition, Vol. I, p. 158-159.</ref><ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. I, p. 310-312.</ref><ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. III, p. 619-620.</ref>

===Birth and Parentage===
He was born about 1340 in Brabant, Belgium [2, 7]. Birthdate is calculated from his being aged 2 in 1342.
He was son and heir of his father, Sir John de Beaumont, 2nd Lord Beaumont, Earl of Buchan, who was b. Cal 1318, of, Shirwell, Devonshire, England and died 14 Apr 1342 (Age ~ 24 years). His mother was Lady Eleanor Plantagenet, b. Abt 1318, Grismond Castle, Monmouthshire, England and d. 11 Jan 1371-1372, Arundel, Sussex, England (Age ~ 54 years). His parents were married before Jun 1337 [2, 9, 10, 11, 12]. Sir John was Lady Eleanor's first husband. [2, 9]<ref>Family Search Commnity Trees, Europe: Royal and Noble Houses of Europe </ref>
