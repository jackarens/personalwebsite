== Biography ==

*Father: Sir John Edmonstone, Baron Boyne.

*Sir John Edmonstone was born at of Culloden, Scotland.
* He married: [[Stewart-991 | Lady Isabel Euphame Stewart aka de Brus]] (1348 - 26 Oct 1410) , daughter of Sir Robert II Stewart, King of Scotland, Earl of Atholl, Strathearn, 7th Steward of Scotland and Elizabeth Muir, between 5 August 1388 and 1390.

* Child: Sir David Edmonstone (1391-1426).
* Sir John Edmonstone died 1415 at Edmonstone, Newton, Midlothian, Scotland.
