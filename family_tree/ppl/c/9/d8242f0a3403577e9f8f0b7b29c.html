==Biography==

Death and burial of Walter de Burgh:
(Royal Ancestry) Sir Walter de Burgh, 2nd Earl of Ulster, died at Galway Castle 28 July 1271, and was buried in the monastery of Athassel-on-the-Suir, co. Tipperary, Ireland.
In medieval times the priory was known as Athassel Priory of Edmund the King. It is now ruins with some graves being marked. It is reported that one tomb exists of a 13th century knight that may be that of Walter de Burgh.
