== Biography ==John, Lord Beaufort & Nogent Lartauld was born before May 1286.<ref name="2plan">Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry, p. 422-424.</ref>
He married Alix de Joinville, daughter of Jean 'the Chronicler' de Joinville, Seneschal & Governor de Champagne and Alix de Reynel, before July 1312; They had no issue.<ref name="2plan"/>
John, Lord Beaufort & Nogent Lartauld died circa 28 September 1318 at France.<ref name="2plan"/>
