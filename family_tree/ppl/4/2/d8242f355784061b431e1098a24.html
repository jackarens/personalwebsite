== Biography ==Anabel Chaucomb was born circa 1210 at Arundel, Sussex, England, the daughter of Sir Robert de Chaucomb and Juliane de Cornhill.<ref name="2royal">Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. IV, p. 671-672.</ref>

=== Marriage & Children ===:She married, firstly, Sir Gilbert de Segrave, son of Sir Stephen de Segrave, Lord Segrave, Sheriff of Buckinghamshire, Bedfordshire, Worcestershire, Leicestershire, & Northamptonshire, Justiciar of England and Rohese Despenser, before 30 September 1231.<ref name="2royal"/>
:She married, secondly, Sir Roger de Somery, son of Sir Ralph de Somery, Baron Dudley and Margaret le Gras, in 1254. They had 2 sons:<ref name="2royal"/>

# Sir Roger
# Sir John Percival

=== Death ===:Anabel Chaucomb died circa 16 December 1282 at Staffordshire, England.<ref name="2royal"/>
