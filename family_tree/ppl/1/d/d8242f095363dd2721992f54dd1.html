=== Biography ===

Alan Stewart, 3rd Earl of Menteith. d. before 13 March 1309
*Alan Stewart, 3rd Earl of Menteith was the son of Alexander Stewart, 6th Earl of Menteith and Maud (?).1 He married Marjory (?).1 He died before 13 March 1309.
*He gained the title of 7th Earl of Menteith circa 1300. As a supporter of Robert Bruce, he was imprisoned by the English and died in captivity.

Child of Alan Stewart, 3rd Earl of Menteith and Marjory (?)

*Mary Stewart, Countess of Menteith d. b 29 Apr 1360
