== Biography ==
Eleanor was born about 1251, to John Warenne[http://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Warenne-1] and Alice de Lusignan[http://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Lusignan-1]. She married Henry Percy[http://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Percy-35] and was the mother of Henry de Percy, 1st Baron Percy of Alnwick. She passed away about 1282.

==Burial==(Royal Ancestry) Eleanor and her husband Henry de Percy were both buried at Sallay Abbey in Yorkshire. "Sallay" was the name of the abbey in medieval times but in later years its name became Sawley Abbey, and due to boundary changes became located in Lancashire. It is now ruins in the care of English Heritage.
Historic details about Sallay/Sawley Abbey were provided by an e-mail dated June 26, 2006 to Alton Rogers from Ella Hatfield, Clerical Officer of Craven (Dist.) Museum and Gallery in Skipton, N. Yorkshire. Sawley is about 15 miles SW of Skipton Castle.
