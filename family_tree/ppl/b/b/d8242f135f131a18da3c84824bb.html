== Biography ==
Isabel married firstly Malcolm Drummond of Drummond, son of John Drummond of Stobhall & his wife Mary de Montefichet.
She married secondly as his first wife, Alexander Stewart, illegitimate son of Alexander Stewart of Badenoch.
