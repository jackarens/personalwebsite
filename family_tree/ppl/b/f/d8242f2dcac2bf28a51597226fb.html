==Biography==
Richard was born about 1247 and died about 1256. He was the son of Henry lll Plantagenet, King of England and Eleanor of Provence (b. 1217 - d. 24 Jan 1291) married, 14 Jan 1236, Canterbury Cathedral, Kent, England
Richard Plantagenet of England (c.1247 - 1256) son of Henry III, King of England. Buried in tomb, with the other young children of Henry III, in the South Ambulatory of Edward the Confessor's Chapel, Westminster Abbey. <ref>Pinterest/Pin/Plantegenet</ref>
(Royal Ancestry) Richard of England, born about 1247, died 29 August 1250, and was buried at Westminster Abbey.
