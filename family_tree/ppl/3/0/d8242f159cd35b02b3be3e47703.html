Narrative

== Biography ==
Walter was born about 1260, the son of Sir Thomas de Somerville of Linton & Carnwath. He was an ally of King Robert Bruce and married Giles, the daughter and heiress of Sir John Herring. They had three sons, James, Thomas, and Richard.
