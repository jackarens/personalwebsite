== Biography of Elizabeth EGLINTON ==
*Elizabeth Heiress of EGLINTON,Born ABT 1343 in Eglinton Castle, Kilwinning, Ayrshire, Scotland
*Elizabeth Eglinton; m. Sir John Montgomery, d. before July 1401, captured Sir Henry "Hotspur" Percy at the battle of Otterburn 1388. [Magna Charta Sureties]
*Elizabeth was child of Sir Hugh by his first wife Agnes Mure, not daughter of Egidia [as originally published]. [Magna Charta Sureties]
