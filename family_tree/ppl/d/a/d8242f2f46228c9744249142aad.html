Narrative

Queen consort of Castile and Leon, Countess of Ponthieu
Eldest daughter of Simon Dammartin and Marie of Ponthieu, granddaughter of Alberic II and Mahaut de Clermont, William IV of Ponthieu and Alys, daughter of King Louis VII of France.
Jeanne, also known as Joan, would be a very wealthy woman, for her inheritance of Pontheiu from her mother and Aumale from her father's family.
