== Biography ==Esmé Stuart, 1st Duke of Lennox (c1542-1583) was the son of John Stuart, 5th Seigneur d'Aubigny and Anne de la Queuille. He married Catherine de Balsac, daughter of Guillaume de Balsac, Seigneur d'Entragues et Marcoussins and Louise d'Humieres, circa 1572. He succeeded his father as 6th Seigneur d'Aubigny, de Berry on 31 March 1567. In September 1579 he went to Scotland, after being brought up in France. He was created 1st Earl of Lennox on 5 March 1579/80. In 1582 he worked for the restoration of Mary Queen of Scots, but was exiled.He died on 26 May 1583 at Paris, France, from a fever.
