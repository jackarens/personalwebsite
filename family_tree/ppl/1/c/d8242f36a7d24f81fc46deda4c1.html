==Biography==
'''Father''' Sir William Marshal, 4th Earl of Pembroke, Marshal of England, Sheriff of Gloucestershire & Sussex, Constable of Lillebonne2,4,5,6,7,8,12,10,11 b. 1146, d. 14 May 1219

'''Mother''' Isabel de Clare2,4,5,6,12,11 b. 1173, d. 7 Mar 1220
She married Hugh le Bigod, Magna Carta Surety, 5th Earl of Norfolk and Suffolk, son of Sir Roger le Bigod, Magna Carta Surety, 4th Earl Norfolk, Keeper of Hertford Castle and Ida de Tony, in 1207; Married before Lent.
They had 4 sons (Sir Roger, 6th Earl of Norfolk; Sir Hugh; Sir Ralph; & William) and 1 daughter (Isabel, wife of Gilbert de Lacy & of Sir John FitzGeoffrey).2,3,4,5,6,8,9,10,11
Maud Marshall married Sir William de Warren, 6th Earl Warren and Surrey, Sheriff of Surrey, 6th Lord of Lewes, son of Hameline, 5th Earl of Surrey, Vicomte de Touraine and Isabel de Warren, before 13 October 1225;
They had 1 son (Sir John, 7th Earl of Surrey) and 1 daughter (Isabel, wife of Sir Hugh d'Aubeney, 5th Earl of Arundel).2,4,5,6,7,9,11 Maud Marshall died on 27 March 1248; Buried at Tintern Abbey, Monmouthshire.2,3,4,5,6,11
