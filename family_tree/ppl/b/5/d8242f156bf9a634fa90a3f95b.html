== Biography of Sir Thomas de Somerville==
*Sir Thomas de Somerville was the son of Sir William de Somerville. He married, firstly, Janet Stewart, daughter of Sir Alexander Stewart of Darnley. He died in December 1444.*He lived at Linton, Roxburghshire, Scotland.2 He lived at Carnwarth, Lanarkshire, Scotland. In 1423 he was an ambassador to London to treat for the ransom of James I, King of Scotland.
