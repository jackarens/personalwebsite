== Biography ==

*Janet Douglas d. after August 1489
*Janet Douglas married Thomas Erskine, 2nd Lord Erskine, son of Sir Robert Erskine of that Ilk, 1st Lord Erskine and Lady Elizabeth Lindsay, before 1445.
*She died after August 1489. From before 1445, her married name became Erskine.
