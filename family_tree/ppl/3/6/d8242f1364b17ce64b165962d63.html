: Note <span id='N1325'>N1325</span>: Margaret Stewart, Countess of Angus, succeeded to the title of 3rd Countess of Angus in 1361, suo jure. As a result of her marriage, Margaret Stewart, Countess of Angus, was styled as Countess of Mar. She resigned as Countess of Angus, in favour of the father of her children, George Douglas, reserving for herself the frank tenement thereof for life on 9 April 1389.
