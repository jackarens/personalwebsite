== Biography =='''Father''' Sir Hugh le Despenser "the younger", 2nd Lord le Despenser, b. c 1290, d. 24 Nov 1326

'''Mother''' Eleanor de Clare b. c 23 Nov 1292, d. 30 Jun 1337

Isabel le Despenser was born circa 1313 (age 8 in 1321).
She married Sir Richard de Arundel, 9th Earl of Surrey, 10th Earl Arundel, son of Sir Edmund FitzAlan and Alice de Warren, on 9 February 1321 at King's Chapel, Havering atte Bower, Essex, England.

They had 1 son (Sir Edmund).
The marriage was annulled on 4 December 1344, and the issue bastardized (and so disinherited).
Isabel le Despenser died after 1356 and was buried in the Chapter House of Austin Friars, London.

'''Magna Carta ancestry
Isabel le Despenser was descended from 6 Magna Carta sureties. In fact she and [[Clifford-59|Idoine de Clifford]] were genealogical doubles. Their mothers were 1st cousins, by descent from the Clare-Lacy marriage. Their fathers were 2nd cousins, by descent from the Fitzgeoffrey-Bigod marriage. So the two ladies had the same Magna Carta blood in the same quantities.
