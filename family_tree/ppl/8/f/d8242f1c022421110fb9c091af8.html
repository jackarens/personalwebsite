==Disputed Parentage==
Scots Peerage, Vol. 2, p 258 under Comyn of Buchan, states that John Comyn had no issue by his wife, [[MacDuff-28|Isabel]] daughter of Duncan (McDuff) Earl of Fife.
It does not say that [[MacDuff-28|Isabel]], daughter of Duncan, had no children, but there is no mention of any other spouse. The Wikitree article for [[MacDuff-28|Isabel]], daughter of Duncan, mentions no daughters. This profile has been disconnected from her and her husband [[Comyn-106|John Comyn]]. Complete Peerage, Vol. 2 p 375 says [[Comyn-106|John Comyn]] died "s.p.m." which means without male children. Neither it nor Scots Peerage say that he had no daughters by other women.
