==Biography==

'''Father''' Sir Roger de Somery b. c 1208, d. c 26 Aug 1273

'''Mother''' Anabel Chaucomb b. c 1210, d. c 16 Dec 1282

Sir Roger de Somery, Baron Dudley was born on 24 June 1255 at of Dudley in Sedgley, Handsworth, King's Swinford, Meer in Forton, & Rowley Regis, Staffordshire, England; Age 18 in 1255.<ref>Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry, p. 137</ref>
Sir Roger de Somery, Baron Dudley married Agnes; They had 2 sons (Roger; & Sir John, Lord Somery) & 2 daughters (Joan, wife of Thomas Botetourt; & Margaret, wife of John de Sutton).<ref> Douglas Richardson, Magna Carta Ancestry, 2nd Edition, Vol. IV, p. 149-150.</ref>
He died on 11 October 1291 at of Staffordshire, England, at age 36; Buried at Dudley Priory.<ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. IV, p. 673-674</ref>
