== Biography ==Margaret le Despenser was born about 1283,{{Citation needed}} daughter of Hugh le Despenser (1262-1326), 1st Earl of Winchester,<ref name="Wikipedia">[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Le_Despenser le Despenser]</ref> and Isabel de Beauchamp<ref>"between 10 Sept. 1285 (record of Highworth Hundred Rolls) and 27 Jan. 1287 (date of fine for marrying without license)." [[#MCA]]</ref> daughter of Sir William de Beauchamp, 9th Earl Warwick, 6th Baron Beauchamp and Maud FitzJohn.<ref name="OurHugh1">Source [[#OurHugh1]]: [http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p368.htm#i11056 Sir Hugh le Despenser, Earl of Winchester], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors & Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref><ref>Isabel had previously been married to "Patrick de Chaworth, Knt., of Kidwelly, Carmarthenshire, Wales.... He died testate shortly before 7 July 1283." ([[#MCA]]).</ref>
Hugh le Despenser and Isabel (Beauchamp) had 2 sons and 4 daughters, including Margaret.<ref name="MCA">[[#MCA]]: Richardson, ''Magna Carta Ancestry'' (2011)</ref>
Margaret was the wife of Sir John, 2nd Lord St. Amand<ref name="OurHugh1" /><ref>married around 1314, see [http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p549.htm#i16487 Margaret le Despenser], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors and Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref>
They had two children:<ref name="Our1">[http://our-royal-titled-noble-and-commoner-ancestors.com/p549.htm#i16487 Margaret le Despenser], "Our Royal, Titled, Noble, and Commoner Ancestors & Cousins" (website, compiler Mr. Marlyn Lewis, Portland, OR).</ref>
* Sir Amaury, 3rd Lord St. Amand, Justiciar of Ireland
* Isabel, wife of Sir Richard de Handlo, & of Sir Robert de Hildesley.
Margaret (Despenser) St Amand died before 25 January 1330.<ref name="Our1" />
