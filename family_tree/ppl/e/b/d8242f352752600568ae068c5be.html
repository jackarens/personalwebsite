==Biography==Sir Stephen de Longespée was the son of William Longespée, Earl of Salisbury and Ela de Salisbury. Hem. betw 8 Feb 1242/3 and 16 Dec 1244 Emeline de Ridelsford (d. betw 18 May 1275 and 19 July 1276) , widow of Hugh de Lacy, Earl of Ulster (d. 1242) and da. and cohrrs. of Walter de Ridelisford. They had two da. Ela and Emeline.

He d. testate bef. 25 June 1260, buried at Lacock Priory, Wiltshire.
