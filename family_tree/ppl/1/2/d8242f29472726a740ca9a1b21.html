[[Category:Quincy-226 Descendants]][[Category:De_Vere-309 Descendants]]{{Magna Carta}}:'''{{#profile:Prefix}} {{#profile:RealName}} {{#profile:LastNameAtBirth}}''' is a descendant of Magna Carta surety barons '''[[de_Vere-309|Robert de Vere]]''' and '''[[Quincy-226|Saher de Quincy]]'''.

==Biography==
: '''Father''' Sir Robert de Vere, 5th Earl Oxford, Baron of Hedingham & Whitchurch, Master Chamberlain of England b. c 1240, d. 25 Aug 1296
: '''Mother''' Alice de Sanford b. c 1230, d. 7 Sep 1312. Of Great Hormead, Hertfordshire, England [among many other properties]. <Ref>''A Vision of Britain through Time''</ref>
: Joan de Vere was born circa 1264 at of Medmenham, Buckinghamshire, England. 2 She married Sir William de Warren, son of Sir John Warren, 7th Earl Warren and Surrey and Alice de Lusignan, in 1284; They had 1 son (Sir John, 8th Earl of Surrey) and 1 daughter (Alice, wife of Sir Edmund de Arundel, 9th Earl of Arundel). Joan de Vere died on 23 November 1293; Buried with her husband before the High Altar at Lewes, Sussex. <ref>''Magna Carta Ancestry: A Study in Colonial and Medieval Families'', vol IV, page 286</ref>
