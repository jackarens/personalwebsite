== Biography ==
Archibald Douglas was born in 1372. He was the son of [[Douglas-404|Archibald Douglas]] and [[Moray-52|Jean Moray]]. He passed away in 1424. <ref>Entered by Sheri Sturm, Jan 23, 2013</ref>
He fought in the Battle of Shrewsbury in 1403 and killed Sir Walter Blount, the carrier of the Royal Standard, and Edmund Stafford, 5th Earl of Stafford.
