== Biography ==
Elizabeth Stewart was the daughter of Thomas Stewart, 2nd Earl of Angus and Margaret Sinclair. She had dispensation to marry Sir Alexander Hamilton of Ballencrieff and Innerwick on 15 September 1381. (1)
