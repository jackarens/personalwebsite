== Sources ==
Findoig (Finval) MacGilmory. Born about 1192 in Argyllshire, Scotland.
Married Sir Duncan Campbell of Lochow, son of Gillespic (Archibald) Campbell of Lochow and Finvol Fraser, about 1210 in Scotland when about 18 years old (Sir Duncan of Lochow was about 52 years old). Sir Duncan Campbell of Lochow b. in 1158 in Lochow, Argyllshire, Scotland.
Issue of Findoig (Finval) MacGilmory and Sir Duncan Campbell of Lochow: i. son Sir Gillespic (Archibald) Campbell of Menstrie b. in 1199; d. about 1280, about 81 years old; m. (1) Margaret Somerville about 1227 in Carnwath, Argyllshire, Scotland when about 28 years old; m. (2) Effrick (Elesick) Campbell (abt. 1200 in Carrick, Argyllshire, Scotland–) about 1229 in Argyllshire when about 30 years old (Effrick (Elesick) was about 29 years old); issue (surname Campbell): Sir Cailein ’Mor’ (Great Colin) of Lochow (1230 in Lochow–1294 in Ath-Dearg (Red Fort) Of Lorn). ii. son Duncan ’Dubh’ Campbell 1st of Strachur b. about 1212; d. in 1280, about 68 years old; m. _____ _____ about 1259 when about 47 years old; issue (surname Campbell): Sir Arthur 2nd of Strachur, Constable of D (abt. 1260 in Strachur, Cowal, Argyllshire, Scotland–1330). iii. dau. Moir Maith Campbell b. about 1216 in Lochow; m. John MacGregor (abt. 1212 in Glenorchy, Argyllshire, Scotland–) about 1242 in Lochow when about 26 years old (John was about 30 years old).
