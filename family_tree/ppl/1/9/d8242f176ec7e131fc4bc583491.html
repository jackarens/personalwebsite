== Biography ==
Scots Peerage <ref name=Paul> Sir James Balfour Paul, Editor, 1908, The Scots Peerage, founded on Wood's edition of Sir Robert Douglas's Peerage of Scotland, Published by David Douglas, Edinburgh, Scotland </ref>, Vol 5, p2, under Innermeath, states : "the wife of [[Ergadia-14|John, Lord of Lorn]] was Joanna, daughter of Thomas de Ysak and Matilda, daughter of King Robert I (Bruce)".Scots Peerage<ref name=Paul/> Vol. 1 p. 3 has a similar statement and gives her death date as July 20, 1353 and her burial location as Dunfermline. Richardson <ref name = Richardson>Douglas Richardson, "Magna Carta Ancestry 1st edition" , Genealogical Publishing Co., Baltimore, 2005. p 779. Also "Royal Ancestry" Vol. 5 p 39. </ref> agrees except for death date, gives approximate birth date, and states that she was buried at Cambuskenneth Abbey, Stirlingshire.
She married, second<ref name = Richardson/>, after Sept 29, 1372, Malcolm Fleming Sheriff of Dumbartonshire.
See the profile of [[Stewart-1170|John Stewart]] for the wording of a marriage dispensation that verifies the connection between him and Robert the Bruce though this couple.
Her name is usually given in 20th century secondary sources as Joanna or Jonet Isaac.
