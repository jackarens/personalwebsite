== Biography ==

*Margaret of Orkney or Margaret Sinclair
*Margaret Sinclair is the daughter of John, Jarl of Orkney and unknown daughter Grahame. She married Sir Simon Fraser of Brotherton and 1st of Lovat, son of Sir Andrew Fraser of Touchfraser, younger and Beatrix le Chene of Duffus. Her married name became Fraser.
