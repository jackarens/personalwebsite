== Biography ==Matthew de Louvain was the son of [[Leuven-14|Godefroi de Louvain]] and [[Hastings-620|Alice de Hastings]].<ref>The Peerage; Mosley, Volume 2, page 2692</ref>
In April 1226, "Matthew de Lovano" paid homage to the king "for the ten knights' fees that Godfrey de Lovano his father held of the king". <ref> Medieval Lands, 2nd Edition </ref>
