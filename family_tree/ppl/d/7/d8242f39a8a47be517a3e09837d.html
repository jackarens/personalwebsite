== Biography ==James Hope was born in 1614.<ref name="Hopetoun">"A History of the Hope Family", Hopetoun House Preservation Trust, http://www.hopetoun.co.uk/History-of-the-Hope-Family.html, accessed 15 February 2015.</ref>
James was the fourth son of Sir Thomas Hope of Craighall, King's Advocate of Charles I.<ref name="Hopetoun" />
James was married to the heiress Anne Foulis and through this marriage he acquired lead mines in Leadhills in Lanarkshire and starting calling himself "of Hopetoun", as Hopetoun was the old name for Leadhills.<ref name="Hopetoun" />
James and Anne had a son John Hope (1650 - 1682) who married Lady Margaret Hamilton and purchased land in Abercorn.<ref name="Hopetoun" />
