{{British Isles 742-1499|title place=England}}
ISABEL de Warenne (-before 23 Nov 1282, bur Marham, Norfolk). The Chronicle of Tintern Abbey, Monmouthshire, names (in order) ”Johannes de Garren comes de Garren et Isabella de Aubeni soror eius et comitissa de Arundel” as the children of “Johanni de Garrene comiti de Surrey” and his wife Matilda Marshal of the Earls of Pembroke.
