== Biography ==:Robert Erskine, 4th Lord Erskine was the son of Alexander Erskine, 3rd Lord Erskine and Christian Crichton. He married Isabel Campbell, daughter of Sir George Campbell of Loudoun and unknown Kennedy, circa 1485. He died on 9 September 1513, killed in action at the Battle of Flodden Field.
:"The Battle of Flodden or Flodden Field or occasionally Battle of Branxton (Brainston Moor) was a conflict between the Kingdom of England and the Kingdom of Scotland. The battle was fought in the county of Northumberland in northern England on 9 September 1513, between an invading Scots army under King James IV and an English army commanded by the Earl of Surrey. It was an English victory. In terms of troop numbers, it was the largest battle fought between the two Kingdoms. James IV was killed in the battle, becoming the last monarch from the British Isles to suffer such a death.":Quoted from Wikipedia [[Wikipedia:Battle of Flodden | Battle of Flodden]]
:He succeeded to the title of 4th Lord Erskine and to the title of 16th Earl of Mar, de jure between 10 March 1507/8 and 10 May 1509.
