== Biography ==Hugh Swynford was the child of [[Swynford-39|Sir Thomas Swynford]] and [[Arderne-67|Nicola de Arderne]].
He was a knight in the service of John of Gaunt where he met his wife, Katherine de Roet.
He died at a battlefield in France of dysentery. It is also suggested that he was poisoned by one of John of Gaunt's retainers.
