== Biography ==
Helen/Ellen is a daughter of Sir Archibald Campbell of Lochawe.<ref>[[#S-1]] Balfour Paul; Vol 5, page 339</ref> Helen was born about 1357 and died after 1434.
She first married, before 1369, John Macdonald, son of John Macdonald, Lord of the Isles and Amie MacRuari. She married secondly (dispensation to recontract marriage 30 Mar 1373) Duncan, 8th Earl of Lennox, son of WALTER de Fasselane Earl of Lennox & his wife Margaret of Lennox.
