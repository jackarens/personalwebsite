== Biography ==
Sir Aymer de Maccuswell (or de Maxwell<ref name=Fraser>Sir William Fraser, ''The Book of Carlaverock'' (Edinburgh: unknown, 1873), p. 64-80, digital images, https://archive.org/stream/bookofcarlaverv100fras#page/64/mode/1up. E. Pauley & Co, 1916), p. 7, digital images, https://archive.org/stream/maxwellhistoryge00hous#page/7/mode/1up. Internet Archive (http://archive.org : accessed 28 October 2016).</ref> He was also Chamberlain of Scotland circa 1258-60.<ref name=Paul/>, Sheriff of Dumfiesshire & Peebleshire, and Justiciar Galloway<ref name=Paul/><ref name=Burke/>
Alternatively, Burke reports Emerus was the son, not brother of Herbert Maccuswell.<ref name=Burke>John Bernard Burke, ''A Genealogical and Heraldic Dictionary of the Peerage and Baronetage of the British Empire'' (London:Henry Colburn, 1845), p. 666, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=ZfdRAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA666.
