You may note some consideration of this on the Profile for Andrew Leslie but in short: He is, by all sources, stated to have married Mary Abernethy. However the date of the marriage is not clear. Leslie, the family historian, claims it to be 1313 but does not provide a source document other than a reference to a "marriage-agreement". Andrew was clearly not of age in 1296, as Norman is the only Leslie on the Homage Rolls, but must have been of age in 1317 when he, and his wife, act in relation to her lands at Cairney. Andrew dies in 1324 and his eldest son inherits, apparently, of age, thus must have been born c. 1300 and the same for the marriage. Any thoughts?
