Narrative

Agnes Hepburn was the daughter of Sir Adam Hepburn of Hailes and Janet Borthwick.<ref name="Mosley">Mosley, Charles, editor. Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, 3 volumes. Wilmington, Delaware, U.S.A.: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 2003. Volume 1, page 572.</ref>
A contract for the marriage of Agnes Hepburn and Alexander Home, Master of Home was signed on 2 February 1448.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>

Her married name became Home.
