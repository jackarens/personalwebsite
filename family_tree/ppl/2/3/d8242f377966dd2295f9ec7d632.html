== Biography ==After the death of Henry III, duke of Brabant, Henrik vied for the guardianship of Brabant as uncle of the young children, but was thwarted by duchess Aleidis.<ref>Jan van Heelu, Rymkronyk betreffende den slag van Woeringen van het jaer 1288. (ca. 1288-1294), In: Rymkronyk van Jan van Heelu betreffende den slag van Woeringen van het jaer 1288 (1836), http://www.dbnl.org/tekst/heel001jfwi01_01/heel001jfwi01_01_0011.php, p.8</ref> In 1279 Henrik, landgrave of Hesse renounced all his rights on the duchy of braban in favour of Jan I, witnessed by his sisters Mathilde, countess of Artois and Saint-Pol, and Beatrix, lady of Kortrijk, and his nephews Robert, count of Artois, Jan of Hainaut and Henrik, lord of Herstal.<ref>A. Verkooren, Inventaire des chartes et cartulaires des duchés de Brabant et de Limbourg et des pays d'Outre-Meuse, Première partie: chartes originales et vidimées, Tome I, nr. 104.</ref>
