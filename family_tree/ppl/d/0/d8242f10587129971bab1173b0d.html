== Biography ==*Catherine Maxwell was the daughter of Herbert Maxwell, 1st Lord Maxwell and unknown daughter Herries.She married Gilbert Kennedy of Dunure, 1st Lord Kennedy, son of Sir James Kennedy of Dunure, younger and Lady Mary Stewart, circa 1440.*She was also known as Katherine Maxwell. From circa 1440, her married name became Kennedy.2
