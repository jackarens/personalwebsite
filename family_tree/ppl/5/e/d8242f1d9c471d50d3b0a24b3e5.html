== Biography ==
Janet Stewart was the daughter of Matthew Stuart, 11th Earl of Lennox and Margaret Lyle.She married Ninian Ross, 3rd Lord Ross of Halkhead, son of John Ross, 2nd Lord Ross of Halkhead, before 15 November 1515.1 She died before 12 December 1523.She is more usually described as a daughter of John, 1st Earl of Lennox, but Cockayne concludes she is more likely a daughter of Matthew "for chronological reasons."
