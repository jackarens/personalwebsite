== Biography ==
:Arthur Erskine of Blackgrange was the son of John Erskine, 5th Lord Erskine and Lady Margaret Campbell. He married Magdalen Livingston, daughter of Alexander Livingston, 5th Lord Livingston, on 7 January 1561/62. He died circa January 1570/71, without issue.<ref>Entered by Daniel Thompson.</ref>
