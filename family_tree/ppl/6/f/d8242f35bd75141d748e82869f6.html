== Biography ==Ela Beauchamp was born circa 1230 at Bedford, Somerset, England, the daughter of Sir William de Beauchamp and Ida Longespee.<ref name="2plan">Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry, p. 528-529.</ref><ref name="3magna">Douglas Richardson, Magna Carta Ancestry, 2nd Edition, Vol. II, p. 476-477.</ref><ref name="4plan">Douglas Richardson, Plantagenet Ancestry: 2nd Edition, Vol. III, p. 428-429.</ref>

=== Marriage & Children ===:She married Sir Baldwin Wake, Lord Wake of Liddell, son of Hugh Wake, Sheriff of Yorkshire, Constable of Scarborough Castle, Baron of Bourne and Joan de Stuteville, circa 1254.
