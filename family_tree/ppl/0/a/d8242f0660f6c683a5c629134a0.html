=== Biography of Margaret Countess of Buchan===
*Margaret, Countess of Buchan was the daughter of Fergus, 4th Earl of Buchan. She married, secondly, William Comyn, Earl of Buchan, son of Richard Comyn, Lord of Tynedale and Hextilda (?), before 1210.1 She died between 8 April 1242 and 1244.
*She succeeded to the title of 5th Countess of Buchan [S., c. 1115] before 1199. From before 1210, her married name became Comyn.
Children of Margaret, Countess of Buchan and William Comyn, Earl of Buchan

*Elizabeth Comyn d. 1267
*Alexander Comyn, 6th Earl of Buchan d. b 6 Apr 1290
