== Biography ==
Sophia Berthout, Vrouwe van Mechelen, was the daughter and heir of [[Berthout-13|Floris Berthout, Heer van Mechelen]] and his wife [[Mark-526|Mechteld von der Marck]]. Her birth date is not recorded but given her eldest child was born about 1320, then a date of around 1303 is a possibility.
In 1311 a marriage contract was made between Renaud, son of the count of Gelre and Sophia, the daughter of Floris Berthout.<ref>A. Verkooren, Inventaire des chartes et cartulaires des duchés de Brabant et de Limbourg et des pays Outre Meuse. Deuxième partie. Cartulaires. Tome I, 1961, p. 229.</ref>
Pope Clement V issued a dispensation for her marriage to Reinald van Wassenberg or van Gelre, because they were related in the 4th degree of consanguinity, on 13 May 1311, but it's not clear whether this was before or after their marriage. He succeeded as Reinald II, Graaf van Gelre (Guelders) in 1326.

They had four daughters;
#Margareta, born about 1320;
#Mechtild, born about 1325;
#Elisabeth or Isabella, born before 6 February 1328
#Maria, born between March 1328 and 6 May 1329.

Sophia died on 6 May 1329 and was buried in Cloister of 's-Gravendael.
