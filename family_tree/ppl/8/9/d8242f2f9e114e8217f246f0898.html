== Biography ==
Infanta doña Maria Fernandez de Castilla y León, was the tenth and youngest child of Fernando III, King of Castile and Leon, and his first wife Elisabeth von Staufen. Maria was born in early 1235 and probably died in November 1235 as she is mentioned as being buried about the same time as her mother, who died on 5 November 1235. Maria was buried in Monastery of San Isidro in Leon.

== Sources ==*Cawley, Charles, 'Castile & Leon, Counts & KIngs: Chapter 4A - Kings of Castile & León 1217-1369' version 3.0, 17 June 2014 in ''Medieval Lands: A prosopography of medieval European noble and royal families'', (http://fmg.ac/Projects/MedLands/CASTILE.htm#_Toc359935767 : viewed 31 May 2015)
