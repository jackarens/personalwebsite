== Biography ==Herbert was the 10th Lord of Caerlaverock.<ref name=Fraser>Sir William Fraser, ''The Book of Carlaverock'' (Edinburgh: unknown, 1873), p. 121-124, digital images, https://archive.org/stream/bookofcarlaverv100fras#page/121/mode/1up. Internet Archive (https://archive.org : accessed 30 October 2016).</ref> He was the last styled as Lord of Caerlaverock, as his son, Hebert was made a peer and the line continued as Lord Maxwell.
Herbert was a son of [[Maxwell-866|Robert Maxwell, Lord of Caelaverock and Mearns]] and his wife [[Forrester-124|Janet Forrester]]. He was a brother of [[Maxwell-364|Margaret Maxwell of Caerlaverock]].

=== Family === He married Katherine Stewart of Dalswinton with whom he had at least 3 children:

# Catherine Maxwell b: ABT 1408 in Linlithgow, West Lothian, Scotland
