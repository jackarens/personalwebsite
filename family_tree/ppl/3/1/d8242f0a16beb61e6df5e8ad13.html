== Biography ==Isabel de Graham, daughter of Sir John Graham of Abercorn and Mary of Strathearn was married to Walter Stewart.
They had three children:
: John Stewart of Ralston
: Andrew Stewart: Egidia Stewart who married 1) Sir James Lindsay of Crawford 2) Sir Hugh Eglinton of that ilk 3) Sir James Douglas of Dalkeith.
