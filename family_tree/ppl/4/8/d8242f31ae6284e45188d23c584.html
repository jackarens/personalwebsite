== Biography ==Mechtild (Mathilde) of Brabant was born circa 1190 at Holland, France, the daughter of Heinrich I 'the Warlike', Duke of Brabant & Lorraine and Maud of Flanders.<ref name="1det">Detlev Schwennicke, Europaische Stammtafeln, New Series, Vol. I/2, Tafel 237.</ref><ref name="3royal">Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. I, p. 500.</ref><ref name="5royal">Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. III, p. 299.</ref>

=== Marriage & Children ===:She married Heinrich II, Count Palatine of the Rhine, son of Henry V 'the Long', Duke of Saxony, Count Palatine and Agnes of Swabia, circa 30 November 1212 at Aachen, Cologne, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.<ref name="1det"/><ref name="5royal"/>
:A settlement for the marriage Mechtild (Mathilde) of Brabant and Florent IV, Count of Holland was made on 5 December 1214. They had 2 sons & 3 daughters:<ref name="1det"/><ref name="3royal"/><ref name="5royal"/><ref>Douglas Richardson, Royal Ancestry, Vol. III, p. 187.</ref>
