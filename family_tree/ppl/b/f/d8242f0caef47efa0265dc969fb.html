== Biography ==
Catherine Seton was the daughter of Sir William Seton of Seton and Janet Dunbar.<ref name="Mosley">Mosley, Charles, editor. Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, 3 volumes. Wilmington, Delaware, U.S.A.: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 2003. Volume 1, page 1285.</ref>
She married, firstly, Herbert Maxwell, 1st Lord Maxwell, son of Sir Herbert de Maxwell and Katherine Stewart.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
She married, secondly, Sir Alan Stuart of Darnley, son of Sir John Stewart, 1st Seigneur d'Aubigny and Elizabeth of Lennox, circa 1429.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
Her married name became, Maxwell<ref name="Mosley"> </ref> then from about 1429, her married name became Stuart.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
