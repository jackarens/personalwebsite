== Biography =='''John Hamilton of Broomhill''' was named as eldest natural son of Lord Hamilton in a charter of 4 Feb 1473/4,<ref>from which we may deduce that an earlier illegitimate son James was dead by then</ref> at which date he was at least old enough to have 2 named younger brothers (though not necessarily by the same mother).
The charter (quoted by Anderson, in translation) named Janet Calderwood as his mother, and granted and entailed lands at Machan Muir (near Larkhill), including a main residence at Broomhill, par. Dalserf, Lanarkshire;<ref>http://www.visionofbritain.org.uk/descriptions/102920</ref> with further lands a few miles away at Silvertonhill, in the barony of Hamilton, with "custody" of Cadzow castle.<ref>Silvertonhill is a mile north of Cadzow close to the centre of modern Hamilton.</ref>
He was legitimated by James IV on 20 January 1512/3, becoming presumptively 2nd in line of succession to his half-brother the 1st Earl of Arran.
On the matter of his marriages and descendants, the secondary sources are in irreconcilable conflict. There is confusion with his son John, and his grandson John, if he had one, and his nephew John, and maybe every other John Hamilton in Scotland.
