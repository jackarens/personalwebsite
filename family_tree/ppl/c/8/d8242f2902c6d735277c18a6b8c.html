== Biography ==
'''Marguerite de France''' (1277/83 - 14 Feb 1318 Marlborough Castle, Wiltshire),[http://fmg.ac/Projects/MedLands/ENGLAND,%20Kings%201066-1603.htm#_ftnref736] was the second wife of Edward I of England. They had three children before he died. Widowed at 26, she never remarried ... and died about ten years later.<ref>see also:* Roberts, G.B. (2008). The Royal Descents of 600 Immigrants to the American Colonies or the United States. Baltimore: Genealogical Publishing Co.
* Roberts, G.B. (2009). Ancestors of American Presidents, (pp. 352). Boston: New England Historic Genealogical Society.</ref>
bur. Greyfriars Church, Newgate, London.[http://fmg.ac/Projects/MedLands/ENGLAND,%20Kings%201066-1603.htm#_ftnref736]
