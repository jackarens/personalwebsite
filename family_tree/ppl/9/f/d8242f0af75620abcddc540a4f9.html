== Biography==Second wife of Robert the Bruce, crowned Queen of Scots with her husband in 1306.
Sources describe little of her life. She was imprisoned by the English,along with her daughter and two of Robert Bruce's sisters on Nov. 7, 1306, but
was released in Jan. 1315.
Attributed son Walter is not listed by any source and likely bogus. Daughter Elizabeth is not listed in Richardson.
