== Biography ==
Sir John Maxwell of Pollok was the son of [[Maxwell-1731|Sir John Maxwell of Pollok]].<ref name=Burke>John Bernard Burke, ''A Genealogical and Heraldic Dictionary of the Peerage and Baronetage of the British Empire'' (London:Henry Colburn, 1845), p. 667, digital images, https://books.google.com/books?id=ZfdRAAAAcAAJ&pg=PA667. Google Books (https://books.google.com : accessed 3 November 2016).</ref> He married, firstly, [[Lindsay-315|Elizabeth de Lindsay]], daughter of Sir James de Lindsay of Crawford and Egidia Stewart.<ref name=Burke/><ref name=Fraser1/> He married, secondly, Elizabeth de Saint Michel.<ref name=Fraser1>Sir William Fraser, ''Memoirs of the Maxwells of Pollok'' (Edinburgh: privately printed, 1863), p. 11-16, digital images, https://archive.org/stream/memoirsofmaxwev100fras#page/11/mode/1up. Internet Archive (https://archive.org : accessed 1 November 2016).</ref>
The children of Sir John Maxwell of Pollok and Elizabeth de Lindsay are:

* [[Maxwell-184|Egidia Maxwell]]* [[Maxwell-4471|Sir John Maxwell of Pollok]], who succeeded him<ref name=Fraser1/> and was given the lands of Nether-Pollok<ref name=Burke/>
* William Maxwell of Aikenhead<ref name=Fraser1/>
* Janet Maxwell, married Thomas Murray or Culbyne<ref name=Fraser1/> * [[Maxwell-862|Sir Robert Maxwell of Calderwood]]<ref name=Fraser1/><ref name=Burke/>* [[Maxwell-864|Agnes Maxwell]], born before 1358, married Gilbert Kennedy of Dunure<ref name=Fraser1/>
