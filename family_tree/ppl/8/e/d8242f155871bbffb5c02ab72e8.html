==Biography of William Somerville==
*William Somerville, 1st Lord Somerville, b. before 1400, d. 20 August 1456
*William Somerville, 1st Lord Somerville was born before 1400. He was the son of Sir Thomas de Somerville and Janet Stewart. He married Janet Mowat, daughter of Sir John Mowat, in 1428/29 in a secret marriage, against his parent's wishes. He died on 20 August 1456 at Cowthally Castle, Lanarkshire, Scotland, from a surfeit of fruit.*He gained the title of 1st Lord Somerville [Scotland] between 28 June 1445 and 3 July 1445.
