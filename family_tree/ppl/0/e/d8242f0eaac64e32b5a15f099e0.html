== Biography ==
Christian Seton was the daughter of Sir John Seton of Seton and Katherine Sinclair.<ref name="Mosley">Mosley, Charles, editor. Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, 3 volumes. Wilmington, Delaware, U.S.A.: Burke's Peerage (Genealogical Books) Ltd, 2003. Volume 1, page 1285.</ref>
She married Sir Norman Leslie of Rothes, son of Sir George Leslie of Rothes and Elizabeth de la Haye, circa 2 September 1416.<ref name="Mosley2">Burke's Peerage, Baronetage & Knightage, 107th edition, Volume 3, page 3413.</ref>
From circa 2 September 1416, her married name became Leslie.<ref name="Mosley"> </ref>
