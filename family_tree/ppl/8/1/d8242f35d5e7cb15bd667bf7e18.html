Narrative

<!--{{Magna Carta}} <center>Sir Robert de Quincy is a descendant of Magna Carta surety baron [[Quincy-226|Saher de Quincy]]</center>[[Category:Quincy-226 Descendants]]-->Sir Robert de Quincy, born before 1200, was the younger of two sons called Robert born to Saher de Quincy, the Magna Carta surety, and his wife Margaret de Beaumont.
He was the second husband of Elen "the Elder" ferch Llewlyn, daughter of Llewelyn the Great.

They had 3 daughters:
* Joan, wife of Sir Humphrey de Bohun VI
* Hawise, wife of Sir Baldwin Wake
* Anne, a nun.
In 1254, Sir Robert was granted an annual fair for his manor in Ware, Hertfordshire. He died at a tournament in Blie in August 1257.
