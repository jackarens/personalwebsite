Narrative

== Biography ==Isabelle's year of birth is unknown. She is mentioned in the Treaty of Vendome in March 1227, when she was betrothed to Alfonso, Count of Poitiers. The marriage contract was later broken and they both married others.
She was first married to Maurice de Craon, and they had several children: Amaury, Maurice, Oliver, Margaret and Jeanne.
A charter dated October 1252 records a dispute with Isabella and her husband's will.
Only one year after Maurice's death, Isabella remarried to Geoffery de Rancon. They had no children. Isabelle was granted a payment by her half-brother King Henry III in 1254. After thirteen years of marriage, Geoffrey died.
