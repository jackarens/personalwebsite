=== Death and burial of Sir William de Warenne ===
:: (Royal Ancestry) Sir William de Warenne, 6th Earl of Surrey, died testate in London 27 May 1240, and was buried in the priory church of Lewes, Sussex. This location was the Priory of St. Pancras aka Lewes Priory.<ref>Royal Ancestry D. Richardson 2013 Vol. I p. 364-366</ref>
