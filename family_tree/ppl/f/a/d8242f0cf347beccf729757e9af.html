==Biography==
Marguerite Turnbull was born about 1332 in Darnley, Renfrewshire, Scotland. She was the daughter of unknown father Turnbull.

===Marriage===
Marguerite was married to Sir Alexander Stewart the son of Sir Alexander Stewart.
