== Sources ==
* '''Royal Ancestry by Douglas Richardson Vol. II. p. 118''''''ALFONSO X OF CASTILE-LEON''', King of Castile, Leon, Toledo, Galicia, Seville, Cordoba, Murcia, Jaen. and the Algarve, Knt., son and heir by his father's 1st marriage, born at Toledo 23 Nov. 1221. He married at Valladolid 26 Nov. 1248 '''VIOLANTE (or YOLANDA) OF ARAGON'''. She was born about 1236. They had five sons, and six daughters. By his mistress, '''MAYOR GUILLEN DE GUZMAN''' (died at Alcocer before 1267), he had one illegitimate daughter, Beatris (or Beatriz) Alfonso. By various other mistresses, he also had three illegitimate sons, and two illegitimate daughters. Alfonso was elected King of the Romans in 1257-72. ALFONSO X, King of Castile, Leon, etc., died at Seville 4 (or 21) April 1284. His widow, Violante, died at Roncesvalles in 1300.
