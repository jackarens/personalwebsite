==Biography of Jacoba Douglas==

*Jacoba Douglas d. after 1410

*Father Sir James Douglas d. 1420
*Mother Agnes Dunbar d. b Oct 1378
*Jacoba Douglas was born at of Dalkeith, Scotland. A contract for the marriage of Jacoba Douglas and Sir John Hamilton, 4th Lord Cadzow was signed on 1 November 1388. Jacoba Douglas died after 1410.
