== Biography ==
Katherine Stewart was born about 1366. She was the daughter of King Robert II of Scotland and Joan (Joanna) Isaac

===Marriage===
She married Sir Herbert Maxwell, Kt., of Caerlaverock with whom she bore 7 children.

===Children===
* 1. Margaret Maxwell b: ABT 1386 in Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland.
* 2. Sir Herbert Maxwell, Knight, 1st Baron Maxwell b: ABT 1388 in Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland.
* 3. Aymer Maxwell b: ABT 1392 in Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland.
* 4. Janet Maxwell b: ABT 1397 in Caerlaverock Castle, Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland.
* 5. Robert Maxwell b: ABT 1403 in Linlithgow, West Lothian, Scotland.
* 6. John Maxwell b: ABT 1404 in Calderwood, East Kilbride, South Lanarkshire, Scotland.
* 7. Catherine Maxwell b: ABT 1408 in Linlithgow, West Lothian, Scotland

Some sources only list three or four children
