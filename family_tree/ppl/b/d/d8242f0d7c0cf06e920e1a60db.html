== Biography of Simon Fraser of Brotherton==
*Simon Fraser of Brotherton, Knight, Sheriff of Kincardine 1317, 1st (Fraser) Lord of Lovat (the feudal title), m Margaret dtr of John, Earl of Orkney and Caithness, whose wife may have been the dtr and was the eventual heiress of Sir David Grahame, Lord of Lovat, and with two of his brothers was k 1333 at the battle of Halidon Hill,
