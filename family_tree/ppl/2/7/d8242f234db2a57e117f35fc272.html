===Parents and Siblings===
John was the son of [[Beaumont-91|Sir Henry de Beaumont]], 1st Lord Beaumont, Earl of Buchan, who was born in France Abt 1285 and died 10 Mar 1339/1340, in Loughborough, Leicestershire, England (Age ~ 55 years). His mother was [[Comyn-103|Alice Comyn]], Countess of Buchan, b. in Scotland Abt 1287 and died 3 Jul 1349 (Age ~ 62 years) They were married in Buchan before 4 Jul 1310 [2, 17, 18, 19] <ref name="john"/> John's brothers and sisters comprised the following:

=== Marriage ===
