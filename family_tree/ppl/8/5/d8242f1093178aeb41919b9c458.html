== Biography ==
*Sir Gilbert Kennedy of Dunure was the son of John Kennedy of Dunure. He married Agnes Maxwell, daughter of Sir John Maxwell of Pollok and Elizabeth de Lindsay.*In 1357 he was a hostage to the English for the liberation of King David II.1
