=== Biography of Margaret of Mar===
*Margaret of Mar is the daughter of Donald, 6th Earl of Mar and Helen ferch Llywelyn.
*She married, firstly, John of Strathbogie, 9th Earl of Atholl, son of David of Strathbogie, 8th Earl of Atholl and Isabel of Chilham.

*She married, secondly, Kenneth de Moravia, 4th Earl of Sutherland.
*She was also known as Marjorie. As a result of her marriage, Margaret of Mar was styled as Countess of Atholl on 6 August 1270.
*She was the daughter of [[Mar-90|Donald Mar]] and [[Llywelyn-16|Helen Llywelyn]]. She passed away after 1306.
