'''3rd Laird of Cadzow'''
Sir David Hamilton of Cadzow, was a Scottish nobleman. The son of David fitz Walter fitz Gilbert of Cadzow, he was born at Cadzow Castle, South Lanarkshire.
''David Hamilton was the<ref>[http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Hamilton_of_Cadzow Wikipedia] - David Hamilton of Cadzow</ref> first of the family recorded as formally using the surname Hamilton, appearing in a writ of 1375 as "David de Hamylton, son and heir of David fitz Walter". in 1378, he is styled David de Hamilton, and in 1381 David Hamilton, Lord of Cadzow. It appears that he was the first to use the Baronial designation of Lord.''
David Hamilton married Janet Keith daughter of Sir William Keith of Galston. Janet survived David Hamilton, and next married Sir Alexander Stewart of Darnley.
