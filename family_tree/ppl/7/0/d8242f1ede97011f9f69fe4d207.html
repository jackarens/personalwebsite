Narrative

== Biography ==Janet Calderwood was the mother of [[Hamilton-4469|John Hamilton]] of Broomhill. John was the illegitimate son of [[Hamilton-7026|James Hamilton, Lord Hamilton]]. <ref>John Anderson, Historical and genealogical memoirs of the House of Hamilton; with genealogical memoirs of the several branches of the family (1825), p. 249; scanned image, (https://archive.org/stream/historicalgeneal00ande#page/248/mode/2up).</ref>
Some writers make her also the mother of John's illegitimate brothers Sir Patrick and David, but the authority for this is unclear.
