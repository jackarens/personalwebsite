== Biography ==: Robert de Vere, Knt. 5th Earl of Oxford<ref name=Rich>Richardson, 2013</ref>
:: "Sir Robert de Vere, 5th Earl Oxford, Baron of Hedingham & Whitchurch, Master Chamberlain of England was born circa 1240 at of Castle Hedingham, Earls Colne, Great Bentley, Great Canfield, Helion, Bumpstead, & Ramsey, Essex, England; Age 22 or 23 in 1263. He married Alice de Sanford, daughter of Gilbert de Sanford, Lord Sanford and Lora (Lorette) Zouche, before 22 February 1252; They had 6 sons (Sir Robert, 6th Earl of Oxford; Sir Hugh, 1st Lord Vere; Sir Alphonse; Thomas; Gilbert, a cleric; & Philip, a cleric) and 2 daughters (Joan, wife of Sir William de Warenne; & Hawise). Sir Robert de Vere, 5th Earl Oxford, Baron of Hedingham & Whitchurch, Master Chamberlain of England died on 25 August 1296; Buried at Earls Colne Priory, Essex." <Ref>''Royal Ancestry: A Study in Colonial and Medieval Families'', vol V, page 254</ref>
